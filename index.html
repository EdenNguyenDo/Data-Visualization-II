AUSTRALIA BUSHFIRE - Black Summer

In the Summer of 2019 - 2020, a serious bushfire occurred

and caused severe damage in Australia. This visualisation

aims to provide a deeper insight into the catastrophe.

This plot highlights the variation in forest types across jurisdictions. It helps us to see the distribution of different forest types and understand the spread more effectively. This visualization informs us about the impact of different forest categories on the environment and policy decisions.

This map showcases the distribution of forest areas across different jurisdictions in Australia. The forest categories include native forest, commercial plantations, and other types of forests. Each category is represented with corresponding data for easier comparison and understanding.

This Sankey diagram shows the flow of forest resources and their distribution between different regions. The visualisation gives a clear picture of how forests are divided among jurisdictions and provides insights into the sustainability of forest management across Australia.
